Philip's Favorites

Now that you know what you like, let me give my own personal recommendations that I believe everyone should try! These are cereals that I personaly enjoy with every cereal order, and while that doesn't mean that you'll enjoy them too, I thought it might be fun to let you check them out.

My Favorite Cereals:

Krave:


Eaten by itself or mixed with strawberries, and chex cereal.

Chocolate Mini Wheats:


Eaten by itself or with blueberries. This cereal has unfortunately been discontinued.

Honey Bunches of Oats:


Eaten with any fruit and granola. Optional mix with other cereals such as Special K, Life, Great Grains, or Golden Grahams.

Count Chocula:


Eaten by itself or with blueberries and granola. This cereal is only distributed during the halloween season, but it's best to get it post-halloween on clearance.

Chocolate Chex:


Eaten with chocolate granola, strawberries, blueberries, and peanut butter chex. I recommend Love Crunch brand granola (either chocolate & red berries or chocolate & peanut butter)
