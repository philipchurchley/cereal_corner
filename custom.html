I suggest that you order:

Cheerios

Quantity: 5


Honey Nut Cheerios is a classic cereal that goes great with fruit. Pro tip: look for knockoffs if you prefer a slightly less sweet option.

Chocolate Chex

Quantity: 5


Chocolate Chex are a great addition to any cereal order. They go well mixed, with fruit, or by themselves. Chocolate Chex also serves well as a chocolatey cereal that isn't overly sweet.

Cinnamon Toast Crunch

Quantity: 5


Cinnamon Toast Crunch is a favorite for many because of its great cinnamon sweetness and texture. CTC can make a delicious breakfast, snack, or dessert.

Fruit Loops

Quantity: 5


Fruit loops are perfect for those who like fruity-sweet cereals. Eat Fruit loops go great as breakfast or a snack, achieving great fruity flavor without any real fruit needed.

Frosted Flakes

Quantity: 5


Frosted Flakes are a reasonably sugary cereal that can be paired with fruit to make an energizing start to your day. If you find them too sweet, look for alternative brand options or mix with less sweet granola.

Great Grains

Quantity: 5


If you're looking for a great, healthy mixer, look no further than Great Grains, Cranberry Almond Crunch. Great Grains is a versatile cereal that can be mixed with fruit or other flake based cereals (or eaten by itself!).

Honey Bunches of Oats

Quantity: 5


Honey Bunches of Oats are the base to any great cereal order. You'll find them delicious when eaten as breakfast or a midnight snack. Try mixing them with any cereal and any fruit.

Life Cereal

Quantity: 5


Life Cereal is a great breakfast or midnight snack. Be careful not to add too much milk and soak it, because Life is at its best when it retains some of its crunch.

Lucky Charms

Quantity: 5


Lucky Charms is a wonderful breakfast, sweet treat or snack. Lucky charms is enjoyed by everyone, especially sugary ceral enthusiasts.

Mini Wheats

Quantity: 5


Mini Wheats makes for an exceptional cereal when eaten correctly. Avoid soaking the cereal with too much milk, but make sure to dip each square in the milk before biting to avoid dryness. Fruit is recommended.

Raisin Bran

Quantity: 5


Raisin Bran is perfect for those who like their cereal less sweet, because the raisins prevent the taste from becoming too "boring". For extra texture, try Raisin Bran Crunch.

Reeces Puffs

Quantity: 5


Peanut butter, chocolate, great when separate! But when they combine they make the morning time epic! Reese's Puffs, Reese's Puffs Eat 'em up, eat 'em up, eat 'em up, eat 'em up.

Rice Krispies

Quantity: 5


Rice Krispies are famous for their use in rice krispy treats. These are delicious, but as cereal they taste great with fruit, and you can mix them without overpowering the flavor of any other cereal. Try with yogurt!

Chocolate Cheerios

Quantity: 5


Chocolate Cheerios are a less-sweet chocolatey cereal option. Best paired with fruit, Chocolate Cheerios make a great breakfast or snack.

Special K: Red Berries

Quantity: 5


Special K: Red Berries are perfect for those who enjoy fruit in their cereal but can't guarentee fruit will always be in stock in their fridge. A healthy option to be enjoyed alone, with fruit, or mixed with granola.

Cocoa Krispies

Quantity: 5


Cocoa Krispies make a great mixer with other chocolatey cereals. They can also be enjoyed by themselves, or try them with some fruit or chocolate granola. Try with yogurt!

Cocoa Pebbles

Quantity: 5


Cocoa Pebbles are a best suited for sugary cereal fans that prefer chocolatey cereals. They can also be mixed with other chocolatey cereals to sweeten the overall taste. Try with yogurt!

Special K: Fruit & Yogurt

Quantity: 5


Special K: Fruit & Yogurt is a versatile cereal that can be eaten alone, or mixed with fruit, granola, and/or other cereals. It also serves as both a delicious breakfast and snack.

Golden Grahams

Quantity: 5


If you like the taste of graham crackers, you will love the taste of Golden Grahams. Golden Grahams is a great option to fulfill a sweet tooth without offering overpowering sweetness.

Honey Oh's

Quantity: 5


A less known cereal, Honey Oh's is essentially a sweeter version of Honey Nut Cheerios, with slightly more crunch. A must try cereal that tastes amazing by itself with just milk.

Krave

Quantity: 5


If looking for a sweet treat or delicious snack, a bowl of Krave is a must try. For extreme chocolate cereal enjoyers, note that a double chocolate variant is also available.
